About Michael Do

A little bit about me

I am a fourth-year student at Portland State University studying computer science. In my first year at PSU, I worked as a lab attendant for OIT. My main job was to assist users with technical difficulties and maintain a clean and proper lab. The skills I developed and improved while working as a member of OIT are communication skills, people skills, critical thinking, and problem-solving. I worked with OIT till my third year at PSU, where I got accepted into MECOP and got my first spring-summer internship at Cognex. At Cognex, I worked as a Software Engineer Intern for their application software team. During my internship, I worked on a couple of projects. The first project I worked on was building an image filter for their cameras, and the main project I worked on was automating firmware loading for their newest cameras. Lastly, I worked on minor bugs. Because Mecop offers two six-month internships, I will be interning at Thermo Fisher Scientific in Eugene Oregon spring-summer of 2023.
